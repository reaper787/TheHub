Just Be Funny. Its not that difficult.

But if you want a more detailed explanation, we understand

  1. Come Up With a Scentence (Dosn't Have to be Funny)
  2. Turn it into a premise and a punchline
  3. Establish Expectations
  4. Make the punchline break expectations
  5. Make it specific
  6. Don't Stutter.
  7. Be Confident people are listening. If you're not, they wont want to listen.
  8. Let's take an example scentence.
    1. The oddest thing I've done in my kitchen is wonder what makes a complete meal.
    2. Format it.

      3. What's the weirdest thing you have done in your kichen? Wonder what makes up a complete meal.

    3. That's Not Funny
    4. Make it specific.

      5. What's the weirdest thing you have done in your kichen? Well for me --I know it varies for everyone-- It's stare at a 2 slabs of bread and wonder what can I put between them to make a complete meal.
